About Us

Welcome to the Diabetic Risk Predictor!

Our mission is to provide a simple and efficient way to assess your risk of diabetes using state-of-the-art machine learning models.

This tool is designed to help individuals make informed decisions about their health by offering personalized risk assessments based on their unique health data.

How It Works

Our system uses a machine learning model that has been trained on a large dataset of health metrics. By inputting your health information, such as blood pressure, glucose levels, and other relevant data, the model can predict your risk of developing diabetes.

We have utilized Logistic Regression, a well-known statistical method, to train our model. This ensures reliable and accurate predictions based on the input data.

Our user-friendly interface ensures that you can easily input your data and receive an instant prediction.

Our Team

We are a group of dedicated professionals with expertise in healthcare, machine learning, and software development. Our goal is to empower individuals with the knowledge they need to take control of their health.
